Welcome Inn ...

Experience Palio’s hottest resort

Situated 600 metres from Palio Beach, Welcome Inn offers a garden, two private swimming pools, a terrace and air-conditioned accommodation with a balcony and free WiFi.
The accommodation comes with a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a modern walk-in shower and a hair dryer, while the kitchen has an oven, a toaster and a fridge. Kitchenware is also offered, as well as a coffee machine and a kettle.
Porto Palio beach is 1.8 km from the apartment, while Glastres Beach is 2 km from the property. The nearest airport is Kavala International Airport, 50 km from Welcome Inn.
>Out resort offers two twins villas, the "East Garden" and the "West Garden", having three suites each.
